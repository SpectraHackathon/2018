What is Spectra?

Spectra is an organization that hosts free full-day events, such as hackathons and workshop days, for young women in tech in the San Francisco Bay Area. In July 2016, we hosted our inaugural hackathon with over 200 women and allies at YouTube HQ in San Bruno, CA.

Spectra is all about creating a safe and inviting environment for women in the tech space. We focus on learning new skills and connecting with like-minded people to help young women advance themselves in the tech world. Spectra believes it's important to host events like these to help students and new grads get comfortable using skills that they've learned to make their cool and creative ideas tangible.

Why should you sponsor Spectra?

Partnering with Spectra can greatly benefit your company through:


  • - Recruiting - Gain access to our database of attendee and mentor resumes and work with us to identify talented women in tech for future job opportunities.
  • - Product exposure - Showcase your company products and technologies through mentoring, workshops, and/or prizes.
  • - Branding - Spread the word about your company as a proponent of tech diversity and outreach by being showcased on our website, social media channels, and attendee swag including apparel.
  • - Mentorship - Event sponsors have the unique opportunity to send their own mentors who can actively recruit and promote for the sponsor.

Interested in sponsoring Spectra?

Let us know at hello@sospectra.com and we would be happy to share available sponsorship tiers. Or, if you have something else in mind, we’ll work with you on a sponsorship plan that your interests and needs.
